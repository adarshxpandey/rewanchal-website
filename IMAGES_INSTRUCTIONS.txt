📁 IMAGES SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

Put your images in the "images" folder with these EXACT names:

1. LOGO:
   - File name: logo.png (or logo.jpg)
   - Location: images/logo.png
   - Used in: Header and Footer

2. CEMENT BAG:
   - File name: cement-bag.jpg (or cement-bag.png)  
   - Location: images/cement-bag.jpg
   - Used in: Products section

📂 Folder structure should look like:
rewanchal-cement/
├── website.html
├── images/
│   ├── logo.png

✅ After adding images, just open website.html in your browser!

- Logo should be square or circular for best results
- Cement bag photo should be clear and well-lit
